---
title: "VERI*FACTU in Spain"
---

Enable eInvoicing for Spain to generate compliant invoices and submit invoice records to AEAT.

RNTOR supports VERI FACTU eInvoicing for Spain and helps you stay compliant by:

- Automatically generating eInvoices and credit notes in the Spain-required format
- Registering and storing invoice records and submitting them to AEAT
- Adding QR codes to invoice PDFs that customers receive
- Tracking eInvoice sync status for issued invoices

<Info>
  This feature is currently in beta. Its functionality and appearance are subject to change, and it may not be fully stable.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

### Complete location billing details

Your location billing details must be complete and accurate.

1. Go to **Location settings \> General page \> Billing details** tab.
2. Fill in your location billing details:
   - Legal business name
   - Address, city, postal code, and country
   - Tax ID
   - Contact details: Email is mandatory

<Frame caption="Location billing details">
  ![Location billing details](/images/einvoicing/location-billing-details.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Make sure your billing country is set to Spain.
</Note>

<Note>
  Spain eInvoicing is only supported for invoices in EUR.
</Note>

### Complete customer billing details

Each customer must also have accurate billing details.

1. Go to **Location settings \> Customers** and manage the **Require billing details** setting.
2. To review or update customer details manually, go to the **Customers** page, open the customer profile, and locate **Billing details**.

#### Requirements for companies

For companies, complete billing details are required for eInvoices to be generated and synced.

If company billing details are incomplete or invalid, invoices may remain in **Draft** status with a warning and cannot be issued.

#### Requirements for individual users

For individual members, only fill in billing details if you want their invoices to be generated as eInvoices and synced as well.

## Enable eInvoicing

1. Go to **Location settings \> Billing**.
2. In the **Invoices** tab, enable the **eInvoicing** switch.
3. In the registration section, click **Start verification process**.
4. Complete the registration form with company and legal representative details.
5. Your billing details and signed agreement will be submitted for verification. Verification may take a few business days.

<Frame caption="Enable eInvoicing in Billing">
  ![Enable eInvoicing in Billing](/images/einvoicing/enable-einvoicing-billing.png)
</Frame>

## How eInvoicing works

After eInvoicing is enabled:

- Invoices for customers with valid billing details are converted into compliant eInvoices.
- Customers continue receiving invoice PDFs with a QR code linking to the invoice record.
- Credit notes are also submitted if their original invoice was successfully synced.

<Frame caption="QR code on invoice PDF">
  ![QR code on invoice PDF](/images/einvoicing/verifactu-qr-invoice-pdf.png)
</Frame>

## Track eInvoice syncing

Each invoice has the eInvoice sync status indicated.

<Frame caption="eInvoice synced status">
  ![eInvoice synced status](/images/einvoicing/einvoice-synced-status.png)
</Frame>

If an eInvoice fails to sync, the invoice will not be issued. The system displays **eInvoice sync failed** and shows a warning near the affected invoice.

<Frame caption="eInvoice sync failed example">
  ![eInvoice sync failed example](/images/einvoicing/einvoice-sync-failed.png)
</Frame>

If prompted, update the customer's billing details and click **Issue** again.
