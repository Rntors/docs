## Set up plan sign-up requests to collect membership applications and verify customers

With sign-up requests, users can apply for membership plans, and you can vet them before creating a subscription.

Plan sign-up requests are ideal for:

- Niche or curated spaces that require personal or professional verification
- Verification-based plans that require ID uploads or supporting documents
- Discounted plans for students, nonprofits, or other groups that need eligibility confirmation.

<Info>
  Plan sign-up requests are available only on [specific plans](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans).
</Info>

<Tip>
  For plans that don't require verification, [use the **Immediate** sign-up type](/admin/resource-management/resources/rental-applications-or-immediate-sign-ups) instead.
</Tip>

## Configure plan sign-up requests

1. [Create](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans) or [edit](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans#edit-or-delete-a-plan) a plan.
2. Enable **Sign-up**.
3. Select **Request** as the sign-up **Type**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 7 38 58 PM 1](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-7.38.58-PM-1.png)
</Frame>

4. [Enable the **Support** page](/admin/support/tickets) and set it to **Public** visibility so users can see administrators’ replies.

<Warning>
  If the **Support** page is disabled, the `Apply` button will be greyed out on plans. If the **Support** page is not set to **Public** visibility, users can apply for plans but won’t see administrator responses.
</Warning>

## How users apply for a plan

1. Customers press `Sign up` next to their desired location to view available plans.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 5 57 23 AM 2](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-5.57.23-AM-2.png)
</Frame>

2. Select a plan, set their preferred subscription start date, renewal, quantity, and whether they want to sign up as an individual member or as a business.
3. Press `Apply` to submit their request.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 9 31 46 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-9.31.46-PM.png)
</Frame>

## How administrators manage plan sign-up requests

1. Each plan sign-up request appears as a new ticket in **Support** \> **Tickets**.
2. Administrators manage the request through the support ticket:

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 11 39 15 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-11.39.15-PM.png)
</Frame>

- Communicate with the applicant
- Request verification documents
- Ask follow-up questions

3. If the user is approved, click the user’s name in the ticket and press `Invite as member`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 18 At 1 21 43 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-18-at-1.21.43-AM.png)
</Frame>

4. [Create a subscription](/admin/billing/subscriptions/set-up-subscriptions) for the requested plan for the customer.
5. If the user requested a company subscription, [create a company](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies) first \> [add this user to the company](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-members-and-managers) as a manager \> create a company subscription.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 18 At 12 46 12 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-18-at-12.46.12-PM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  To avoid missing any notifications about plan sign-up requests, [enable notifications](/admin/administration/your-account/notifications/set-up-notifications) about new support tickets.
</Tip>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I add a customer who applied for a booking or subscription as a member or nonmember?">
    When a user applies for a booking or subscription, they are not a member or a nonmember yet. That’s why they don’t appear on the **Customers** page yet.

    1. Click the user’s name from the support ticket to open their profile.
    2. Click `Invite as member` or `Add as nonmember` at the top right.
