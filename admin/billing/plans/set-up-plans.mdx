Create and manage membership plans for long-term or recurring access without a dedicated resource rental

Plans are membership options or packages of services that customers can subscribe to, usually on a long-term or recurring basis. Common examples in coworking spaces include Hot desk unlimited, Hot desk (10 days), or Flex membership.

With plans, you can:

- Structure and present your membership options to customers
- Let customers sign up instantly or submit applications
- Quickly set up new customers for subscriptions, using plans as templates.

## Set up plans

1. Go to **Billing** \> **Plans**.
2. Click `Create plan`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 16 At 6 39 27 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-16-at-6.39.27-PM.png)
</Frame>

### Plan configuration

- **Name** — appears on customer invoices.
- **Cycle** — the interval of plan validity and how often the subscription renews. Available options: 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year.
- **Description** — customer-facing details of what is included.
- **Price** — the fee for 1 cycle.
- **Setup fee** — one-time charge to add to the customer’s first invoice.

<Info>
  The setup fee is taxable but is not affected by discounts or invoice proration.
</Info>

- [**Custom tax rate**](/admin/billing/taxes/custom-tax-rates) — overrides the location’s default tax rate for this plan.
- **Minimum commitment** — the mandatory minimum number of cycles a customer can subscribe for.
- [**Credits**](/admin/billing/credits-and-day-passes/credits) — expiring credits the customer will receive each cycle for bookings or purchases.
- [**Day passes**](/admin/billing/credits-and-day-passes/day-passes) — expiring day passes the customer will receive each cycle for booking hot desks.
- [**Discounts**](/admin/billing/discounts/apply-discounts-to-bookings-and-purchases) — offer discounts on certain services to members subscribed to this plan.
- **Sign-up** — control whether and how customers can see and sign up for this plan.

### Sign-up setting

| Setting | Outcome |
| --- | --- |
| Enable with **Immediate** type | Customers can see this plan and [sign up immediately](/admin/billing/plans/immediate-plan-sign-ups#how-users-sign-up-for-plans) without admin involvement. |
| Enable with **Request** type | Customers can see this plan and [submit a request](/admin/billing/plans/applications-for-plans#how-users-apply-for-a-plan) to sign up. Administrators process requests as support tickets. Useful if you don’t allow new customers to become your members independently. |
| Disable **Sign-up** | The plan should remain visible only for administrators for [creating subscriptions](/admin/billing/subscriptions/set-up-subscriptions). |

- [**Access**](/admin/integrations/access-control) — access control group. Appears if you have the [integration with the access control system](/admin/integrations/access-control).
- [**Printing**](/admin/integrations/printing/ezeep-blue) — printing access group.
- **Exclusive access** — customers on this plan have exclusive access to see or book resources.

3. Tap `Save`.

<Info>
  Plans are designed for access without a dedicated resource. Dedicated desks, offices, or other rentals — whether long-term, recurring, or hourly or daily access — are configured in [Resources](/admin/resource-management/resources/set-up-pricing).
</Info>

## What’s next

After creating your plans:

- Customers can explore available plans and [sign up](/admin/billing/plans/immediate-plan-sign-ups) or [submit requests](/admin/billing/plans/applications-for-plans), depending on your settings.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 5 57 23 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-5.57.23-AM.png)
</Frame>

- Administrators can quickly [set up new members for subscriptions](/admin/billing/subscriptions/set-up-subscriptions), using plans as ready-made templates with predefined pricing, benefits, and access.

## Edit or delete a plan

1. Go to **Billing** \> **Plans**.
2. Press the menu button `...` next to the preliminary [created plan](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans) and tap `Edit` or `Delete`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 6 05 41 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-6.05.41-AM.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Deleting a plan does not affect active subscriptions. If your members still have this plan selected in their subscription, it will continue to function for them.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Deleting a plan is irreversible and cannot be undone. Instead of completely deleting a plan, you can [disable the **Sign-up** property](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans) to make it impossible for users to purchase the plan by themselves. Meanwhile, administrators will still be able to choose this plan for creating subscriptions manually.
</Warning>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How to create a custom plan for one customer?">
    Start [creating the subscription](/admin/billing/subscriptions/set-up-subscriptions) for your customer, keep **Custom** in the **Product** field, and manually type the plan name, price, and other allowances.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I copy the link to all public plans?">
    Go to **Billing** \> **Plans** \> press the menu `...` top right \> **Copy link to all public plans**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 06 17 At 6 08 40 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-17-at-6.08.40-AM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I offer discounts for plans?">
    To offer targeted discounts on specific plans or for selected customers, create [coupons and promo codes](/admin/billing/discounts/apply-discounts-to-bookings-and-purchases#create-promo-codes) of **Subscription items** type.

    [Apply them](/admin/billing/discounts/apply-discounts-to-bookings-and-purchases) directly when creating subscriptions for users and companies, or share the [promo code](/admin/billing/discounts/apply-discounts-to-bookings-and-purchases#create-promo-codes) with customers to redeem the discount on their own when they sign up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How to offer a package of hours for booking rooms?">
    You can use any of the following options:

    - [**Create a membership plan with credits**](/admin/billing/plans/set-up-plans): This is ideal for recurring packages. Customers subscribe to a plan and automatically receive a set number of expiring credits each cycle to use for bookings.
    - [**Create a credit package**](/admin/billing/credits-and-day-passes/packages): This is best for one-off purchases. Customers can buy a one-off package of credits at any time and use them for bookings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do changes to the plan affect existing members' subscriptions?">
    Changes to the plan only apply to new future subscriptions and those existing members that have **Auto-update from plan** enabled in their subscription.

    To apply plan changes to existing members, you have two options:

    - [**Edit their subscriptions**](/admin/billing/subscriptions/edit-a-subscription) individually and apply changes manually, or
    - Turn on **Auto-update from plan** in existing customers’ subscriptions, then make a change to the plan. This way, it will automatically apply to existing members, too.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How to raise the price for all customers who are subscribed to the plan?">
    Ensure the [**Auto-update from plan** setting is enabled](/admin/billing/subscriptions/edit-a-subscription) in all your customers’ active subscriptions who have that plan. Then, edit the plan as needed.

    - Any changes made to pricing, credits, or day passes will apply automatically to their subscriptions with the start of the next billing cycle.
    - Changes to discounts, door access, and exclusive resource access apply immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
