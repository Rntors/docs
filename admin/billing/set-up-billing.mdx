## Add billing details

1. Go to **Location settings > General > Billing details**.

2. Fill out your location billing details:

   * Legal business name
   * Address
   * Tax ID
   * Contact email

   These details appear on invoices and receipts issued from this location and may be used for tax and eInvoicing compliance.

3. If your country requires additional billing or bank account information, complete the relevant fields before issuing invoices.

<Note>
  Changes to billing details apply to newly issued invoices and receipts. Previously issued invoices remain unchanged.
</Note>

## Configure subscriptions settings

1. Go to **Location settings > Billing > Subscriptions**.
2. Choose how subscription billing should run for your location.
3. Set the **Billing cycle anchor** to define when recurring subscription invoices are generated.
4. Configure whether members should be automatically removed or updated when their subscription ends, fails, or changes status.

## Configure invoice settings

1. Go to **Location settings > Billing > Invoices**.

2. If your country supports eInvoicing, enable **eInvoicing** and complete the required verification flow.

3. Choose the **Invoice type** used for invoices issued from this location:

   * **Invoice** — standard invoice title for issued invoices and invoice emails.
   * **Proforma invoice** — invoice and email title used before final issuance or payment.
   * **VAT invoice** — invoice title used when VAT invoices are required.
   * **Tax invoice** — invoice title used when tax invoices are required in your country.

4. Choose the **Numbering scheme** for invoices:

   * **Customer** — each customer has their own invoice sequence.
   * **Location** — invoices issued from this location share one invoice sequence.

5. Use **Advance invoicing** to define how many days before the next subscription period an invoice should be issued.

6. Set the **Payment due** period to define how many days customers have to pay after invoice issuance.

7. Manage **Payment retries and reminders** to control failed-payment reminders and retry behavior.

8. Fill in the **Footer** field to add additional details at the bottom of invoices and receipts, such as payment instructions or bank transfer information.

9. Fill in **Accounting email** if invoices and receipts should also be sent to your finance or accounting inbox.

## Configure taxes

1. Go to **Location settings > Billing > Taxes**.
2. Enable taxes if your location must charge tax on invoices, receipts, bookings, subscriptions, packages, or other purchases.
3. Choose the tax type required for your country.
4. Set the tax rate.
5. Choose whether prices are tax-inclusive or tax-exclusive.

<Frame caption="Tax settings">
  ![Tax settings](/images/billing/tax-settings.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Make sure your tax settings match your legal and accounting requirements before issuing invoices.
</Note>

## Configure currencies

1. Go to **Location settings > Billing > Currencies**.
2. Select the main currency for your location.
3. Add alternative currencies if you need to sell selected services, packages, or bookings in additional currencies.
4. Choose a default alternative currency only if you want it applied to new customers automatically.

<Frame caption="Currency settings">
  ![Currency settings](/images/billing/currency-settings.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  If you only need alternative currencies for specific customers, keep the default alternative currency empty and apply the currency directly to those customers when needed.
</Note>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Our billing details have changed. How do we update invoices going forward?">
    To update billing details for invoices sent to your customers:

```
1. Go to **Location settings > General > Billing details**.
2. Update your legal business name, address, tax ID, or contact details.
3. Go to **Location settings > Billing > Invoices**.
4. Update invoice footer or accounting email settings if needed.

Once updated, newly issued invoices and receipts will use the new billing details. Previously issued invoices remain unchanged.
```

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why do some users not receive invoices automatically?">
    A member or customer may not receive an invoice automatically for several reasons:

```
- **Email delivery issue:** The email address may be incorrect, inactive, or blocked.
- **Spam or junk folder:** The invoice email may have been delivered but filtered by the customer’s email provider.
- **Payment collection paused:** If payment collection is paused, invoices may not be issued or sent automatically.
- **Missing company manager:** If the invoice is issued to a company, make sure the company has an assigned manager or billing contact.
- **Incomplete billing details:** Some invoice or eInvoicing flows require complete customer billing details before issuance.
```

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do billing detail changes affect old invoices?">
    No. Billing detail changes apply to future invoices and receipts only. Issued invoices should keep the billing details they had at the time of issuance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
