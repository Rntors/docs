Learn how to process contracts signed outside the system
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

With the help of [eSignature](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/contracts/esignature), your customers can sign their contracts digitally online. However, if a customer needs to sign the contract manually (physically), you can manually record and upload the signed document into the system.

## Mark the contract as signed

Once the customer signs the contract manually, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Customers** > open the customer profile > **Contracts** page. Alternatively, go to **Billing > Contracts** and use the filters to find the necessary contract.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/external-signature-contracts-list.png"
  alt="Contracts page showing filters used to find a customer contract"
/>
</Frame>

2. Open the necessary contract.

3. In the **Signatories** section, press the **(...)** menu next to the signatory’s name and select `Mark as signed`.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/external-signature-mark-as-signed.png"
  alt="Contract signatory menu showing the Mark as signed action"
/>
</Frame>

4. Press `Upload signed PDF` to upload the final signed document.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/external-signature-upload-signed-pdf.png"
  alt="Signed contract page showing the Upload signed PDF action"
/>
</Frame>

When the contract is marked as signed by all signatories:

* The contract status changes to **Signed**.
* The system automatically creates the customer’s subscription on the selected start date.
* The system [notifies](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/member/account/notifications) the customer.

If any signatory declines the contract:

1. Press the **(...)** menu next to the signatory’s name.
2. Select `Mark as declined`.

The contract status changes to **Declined**, and the customer’s subscription is not created.
