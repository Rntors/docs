Create, issue, and manage digital contracts to formalize customer agreements
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Contracts are individual agreements between you and your customers. The **Contracts** module provides a seamless, centralized way to store and manage all customer agreements in one place.

Contracts are designed to simplify your onboarding process and ensure legal protection:

* When you need members to formally sign off on liability waivers, building rules, or regulations, such as liability for damages or general conduct.
* To require a digital signature *before* a member is allowed to proceed to payment or activate their plan, ensuring compliance precedes financial commitment.
* To attach specific, separate legal agreements to individual membership plans.
* To eliminate the administrative burden of using third-party tools by allowing administrators to create, send, track, and file contracts seamlessly within Rntor.
* To generate contracts that automatically reflect the dedicated resource being leased, such as a specific desk or office, and link the document to the customer.

## Prerequisites

To start using contracts, make sure you’ve completed the following prerequisites:

* Enable the **Contracts** switch on the **Location settings > Contracts** page.
* [Upload a contract template](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/contracts/templates#upload-a-template).
* Create your [membership plans](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/plans), [resources](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/resource-management/set-up), and [one-off services](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/service/shop/one-off-services).

## Create a contract

1. Go to the **Billing > Contracts** page.

2. Press `Create contract` in the top-right corner.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/contracts-create-contract.png"
  alt="Contracts page showing the Create contract button"
/>
</Frame>

3. Fill out the contract details:

* **Customer**
* **Template**
* **Expiration date** — set the deadline for signatories to sign. If the contract is not signed by that date, it will be automatically declined.

<Info>
The expiration date cannot be in the past.
</Info>

* **Items** — add recurring and/or one-off services the customer will receive under this contract.

  * For each item, specify the quantity, adjust the unit price if needed, or apply a coupon discount.

<Info>
You can apply coupons only to recurring items in contracts.
</Info>

If the customer’s contract includes at least one recurring item, configure the following:

* **Start date**

  * You can create backdated contracts, too.

* **End renewal** — select **On date** to specify an end date, or choose **Never** if the contract has no specific end date.

* **Create subscription from contract**

  * If enabled, the customer’s subscription will activate automatically on the selected start date after all parties sign.
  * If disabled, the administrator must manually [create a subscription](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/subscriptions) for the customer.

<Info>
When adding both recurring and one-off items, the one-off items are included only on the customer’s first subscription invoice. They are automatically removed from the subscription after the first billing period.
</Info>

<Tip>
You can also issue renewal contracts to customers with active subscriptions.
</Tip>

If the customer’s contract includes only one-off items:

* **Issue invoice from contract**

  * If enabled, the customer will automatically receive an invoice containing the selected items when the contract is fully signed.
  * If disabled, the administrator must manually [issue the invoice](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/invoicing/issue-invoices) to the customer.

* **Signatories**

  * Enter the signatory’s name, email address, phone number (optional), and party: customer side or location side.
  * You can add unlimited signatories on both sides.
  * Drag and drop the signatories to define the order in which they should receive the contract email for signature when using eSignature.

<Tip>
For individual customer contracts, you can click to prefill the signatory with information from their profile.
</Tip>

<Info>
The contract may have no location signatories, but it must have at least one customer-side signatory.
</Info>

4. Press `Create`. Then review all details before issuing the contract.

## Issue a contract

1. Open the contract and select one of the following options:

   * Press `Issue` to collect the contract signature manually.
   * Press `Issue with eSignature` to collect the contract signature automatically.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/contracts-issue-options.png"
  alt="Contract details page showing Issue and Issue with eSignature actions"
/>
</Frame>

2. With eSignature, the customer receives an email and can sign the contract digitally. Learn how to [issue contracts for eSignature](https://www.spacebring.com/docs/billing/contracts/esignature#issue-contracts-for-esignature).

3. Without eSignature, share the contract with the customer externally for signature. In this case, the contract is not sent automatically.

Customers can always view their contracts on the **Membership > Billing > Contracts** page.

<img
  src="/images/placeholders/contracts-customer-view.png"
  alt="Customer portal Billing page showing the Contracts section"
/>
</Frame>

## Manage contracts

To view all contracts or find a specific customer contract, administrators:

1. Go to the **Billing > Contracts** page.

2. Use the filters to find a specific contract by status or customer.

You can modify or delete a contract while it is in **Draft** status:

1. Open the contract.

2. Click the **(...)** button in the top-right corner.

3. Select `Edit` to adjust the contract details or `Delete` to remove the draft.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/contracts-draft-actions.png"
  alt="Draft contract showing Edit and Delete actions"
/>
</Frame>

<Info>
Deleting a contract is irreversible and cannot be undone.
</Info>

You can also terminate contracts that have already been issued or signed:

1. Open the contract and click the **(...)** button in the top-right corner.

2. Select `Terminate` and choose the termination reason.

<Frame>
<img
  src="/images/placeholders/contracts-terminate.png"
  alt="Issued contract showing the Terminate action"
/>
</Frame>

Upon termination, a confirmation email containing the reason will be sent to the customer.

To track contract analytics, go to the **Analytics > Contracts** page.
