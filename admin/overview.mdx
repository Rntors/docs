Welcome to the Rntor admin portal. Think of it as the digital backend for running your space. Rntor's **admin web portal** is designed for complete desktop management, while the **admin mobile app** will help you handle tasks on the go.

Here is a quick overview of how to get in, navigate the platform, and manage your space with confidence.

## Admin web portal

1. Go to [app.rntor.com](https://app.rntor.com).
2. Enter the email associated with your admin account and verify it with the confirmation code.

Your main control center is the left-side navigation panel. Here is a quick breakdown of where to find exactly what you need.

| Module | What you will do here |
| --- | --- |
| **Locations** | Switch between locations if your network has multiple spaces. |
| **Customers** | Your customer directory. View and manage companies, members, and nonmembers with their info neatly organized by profiles. |
| **Billing** | Your financial hub. Create and manage contracts, subscriptions, invoices, and transactions. Set up your membership plans, packages, and discounts. |
| **Resources** | Your space inventory. Add all rooms, desks, private offices, parking lots, and equipment, and manage their bookings or rentals. |
| **Engagement** | Your community builder. Curate the community feed and events. |
| **Service** | Your extra offerings. Run a shop for additional products and services, maintain a benefits catalog with member perks, and track members' visitors. |
| **Support** | Your help desk. Handle customer feedback and requests, and build out your member guides. |
| **Analytics** | Reports and insights into your space performance. |
| **Settings** | Configure your network, locations, and admin preferences. |
| **Noor AI** | Rntor's AI assistant (beta) to help automate tasks and surface insights. |

<Tip>
  Instantly jump to any page or trigger actions (like "invite user" or "create company") using the command palette.

  - **Windows:** Press `Ctrl + K`
  - **macOS:** Press `⌘ + K`
</Tip>

## Admin mobile app

The Rntor admin mobile app helps you stay on top of your space while away from your desk — approve requests, check bookings, and respond to members from anywhere.

  <Accordion title="Why can't I access my admin account?">
    - **Unable to see admin features?** Double-check that you are signed in to the admin portal and not the member portal.
    - **Lost access?** Regain your admin role by clicking on the settings **\> Merchant wide settings \> Locations \>** `Become administrator` button next to the needed location. Then, go to **Customers \>** open your profile **\>** check the "Activities" section to identify who removed your admin role.
