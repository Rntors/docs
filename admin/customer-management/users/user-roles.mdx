---
title: "User roles"
---

Users can have different roles based on their permissions and activities within the platform. The user's role defines what exactly this user can see and manage in the member web app, mobile app, and Rntor. Users can have either the role of the space operator, taking charge of the location or network, or you can have a consumer role, utilizing your space services.

## Operator roles

**Owner:** full edit rights at the network level. Responsible for managing the entire network and subscription with Rntor.

**Administrator:** full or partial edit rights at the location level. Responsible for managing a specific location.

## Consumer roles

**Member:** customers of your space who have access to the members-only parts of the app and pay you for using your services on a regular basis.

**Nonmember:** occasional customers of your space who arrive for one-off bookings or other services or have been your members in the past.

<Accordion title="What is a difference between member and nonmember?">
  A **member** is a user who belongs to the community of a specific location and can access its member-only features and resources. Members have active subscriptions and access credits, day passes, and view all content within the member web portal and mobile app with "Location members" visibility and higher.

  A **nonmember** is a former member or a user who has made at least one booking, purchase, or order in the location, joined an event, or applied for a benefit.
</Accordion>
