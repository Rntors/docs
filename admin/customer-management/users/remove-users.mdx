title: "Remove users"

Administrators can remove any user from their view and, by removing them, manage these users' roles. Based on the [user role](/admin/customer-management/users/user-roles), the process of removing a user happens on different pages.

## Remove a member

If the user is no longer your [member](/admin/customer-management/users/members) and you need to remove them from the **Customers** page, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Customers** page in the necessary location and click on the necessary user to open their profile.
2. Press the `…` menu top right and select `Remove`, then confirm your choice.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 06 At 12 01 22 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-06-at-12.01.22-AM.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  These steps do not delete the user's account and only make them a [nonmember](/admin/customer-management/users/nonmembers) at this location. Only the user themselves can fully delete their account, as described on the [Manage user account](/admin/customer-management/users/manage-user-account) page.
</Info>

### What happens when you remove a user from the Customers page

- The user becomes a **nonmember at this location** and appears for administrators under **Customers → Nonmembers**.
- The user has the [nonmember's rights](/admin/customer-management/users/nonmembers) at this location.
- If the user was an **Administrator** or **Manager** for this location, those **location-scoped rights are revoked immediately** (`is_administrator`, `is_manager`, and per-location `permissions` are cleared).
- **Access in other locations is preserved.** Removing a user from one location does not affect their membership, admin, or manager rights at any other location of the same merchant.
- **Merchant Owners and Super Admins are never affected** by removal — their access is governed by merchant-wide ownership / super-admin status, not by per-location flags.
- The user's **active personal subscription** at this location is cancelled automatically with today's date set as the end date.
- The user's [default payment method](/admin/billing/payments/payment-gateway) remains attached to the profile for any further charges if needed.
- The full history of the user's **credits, day passes, payments, invoices, bookings, and activities** at this location is preserved and remains visible to administrators.
- The user keeps any [company](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies) membership they had — company membership and any active company subscription are not affected.
- Administrators can [reinvite the user](#reinvite-a-user) at any time without re-entering their information.

## Auto-remove members

RNTOR enables you to automate the offboarding of individual users or companies at a location.

Administrators enable the _Auto-remove members_ property, which allows them to auto-remove members from the **Customers** page once their subscription ends.

### Configure "Auto-remove members" property

1. Administrators go to **Location settings → Billing** page → **Subscriptions** tab.
2. Manage the _Auto-remove members_ switch:
   - If **enabled**, members automatically become nonmembers when their subscription expires.
   - If **disabled**, members don't become nonmembers when their subscription expires. They are still considered your members and are listed on the **Customers** page.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 06 At 12 01 22 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-06-at-12.01.22-AM.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  This setting does not affect administrators. Merchant Owners and Super Admins are never auto-removed.
</Info>

<Info>
  With the _Auto-remove members_ switch enabled, only members whose subscriptions end on the specified date are automatically removed from the **Customers** page. If an administrator cancels a subscription, they must also manually remove the member.
</Info>

### How members are removed

With the _Auto-remove members_ switch enabled, individual members or companies become [nonmembers](/admin/customer-management/users/nonmembers) when their subscription expires at this location. The expiration date depends on the _End date_ set inside the subscription. Administrators can set the expiration date when [creating](/admin/billing/subscriptions/create-a-subscription) or [editing](/admin/billing/subscriptions/edit-a-subscription) the subscription.

A daily background job runs once per day to apply this rule. When the toggle is on for a location, the job will, for each user at that location:

- Move the user to **Nonmembers** at this location only.
- Clear their location-scoped admin / manager rights for this location.
- Leave their access at any other location of the same merchant untouched.
- Skip the action entirely if the user is a Merchant Owner or Super Admin.

### Notes

When a **company subscription** expires, the company and its members **without personal subscriptions** become nonmembers at this location.

However, if a user's **personal subscription** expires but they belong to a **company with an active subscription** at the same location, the user will remain a member.

## Delete a nonmember

Administrators can delete nonmembers from the location by following these steps:

1. Log in to RNTOR.
2. Go to the **Customers** page in the necessary location.
3. Select the **Nonmembers** option in the drop-down list.
4. Switch from **Companies** to **Users** section, then click on the necessary user to open their profile.
5. Press the `…` menu top right and select `Delete`, then confirm your choice.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 06 At 12 03 22 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-06-at-12.03.22-AM.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  If a nonmember is deleted, they can still have access, but they will no longer appear on the nonmembers' list and the administrator won't be able to view their past data or reinvite them to the **Customers**. However, if the user performs any action such as making a booking, payment, or sending a chat message, etc., they will become a nonmember again.
</Info>

## Reinvite a user

RNTOR stores the information of all nonmembers who have ever interacted with your location in the member web portal or mobile app, or used to be a member before. Administrators can easily invite nonmembers to the location so they become members again.

<Info>
  You can manage only those users and companies that are already registered in the member web portal or mobile app, and appear on the **Customers → Nonmembers** tab in RNTOR.
</Info>

To make a user who appears among nonmembers your member immediately without retyping their information, do the following:

1. Navigate to **Customers** → menu `…` top right → **Nonmembers** tab.
2. Select the user whom you want to make a member and press `Invite as member`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 06 At 12 07 47 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-06-at-12.07.47-AM.png)
</Frame>

Once invited, the user receives the email invitation and gains access as a member. The administrators can add the user to the company, create a subscription, and manage other member properties.
