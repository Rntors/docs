title: "remove-users"

Learn how to remove members from the community, delete nonmembers, and reinvite past users back as members.

Administrators can remove any user from their view. Depending on the user's [role](/admin/administration/administrators/permissions), the action takes a different path:

- Removing a **member** moves them to **Nonmembers** — their profile and history are preserved at the location.
- Deleting a **nonmember** removes their profile from the location entirely.

## Remove a member

If a user is no longer your [member](/admin/customer-management/users/members) and you need to remove them from the **Customers** page, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Customers** page in the necessary location and switch the filter to **Members**.
2. Open the user's profile, or use the row's `…` menu directly from the list.
3. Press the `…` menu in the top right of the profile (or in the list row) and select `Remove`.
4. Confirm your choice.

### What happens when you remove a user from the Customers page

- The user becomes a [nonmember](/admin/customer-management/users/nonmembers) and appears under **Customers → Nonmembers**.
- The user's **active personal subscription** at this location is cancelled immediately, with today's date set as the end date. See [Cancel a subscription](/admin/billing/subscriptions/cancel-a-subscription).
- The user's **payment methods** remain attached to the profile for any further charges that may be needed.
- The user's **billing details** remain on the profile.
- The full history of the user's **credits, day passes, payments, invoices, bookings, and activities** is preserved on the profile and continues to be visible to administrators.
- The user keeps access to any [companies](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies) they belong to — their company membership and any active company subscription are not affected.
- Administrators can [restore the user as a member](#reinvite-a-user) at any time without re-entering their information.

<Info>
  If the user was an [administrator](/admin/administration/administrators/administrators), the administrator role is not revoked automatically when they are moved to nonmembers. To revoke admin access, open the user's profile and turn off the **Administrator** switch, or remove them from the administrators list. See [Manage administrators](/admin/administration/administrators/administrators).
</Info>

## Delete a nonmember

Administrators can delete nonmembers from a location when their profile is no longer needed.

1. Go to the **Customers** page in the necessary location.
2. Switch the filter at the top to **Nonmembers**.
3. Open the nonmember's profile.
4. Press the `…` menu in the top right and select `Delete`.
5. Confirm your choice.

The user can still appear again if they perform any action at this location — such as making a booking, payment, or order — at which point a fresh nonmember profile is created.
</Info>

## Reinvite a user

RNTOR keeps every nonmember who has ever interacted with your location available on the **Customers** page. Administrators can promote a nonmember back to **Members** without retyping their information.

<Info>
  You can reinvite only users who appear on the **Customers → Nonmembers** list. Users whose nonmember profile has been [deleted](#delete-a-nonmember) need to be added again from scratch.
</Info>

To make a user who appears among nonmembers your member immediately:

1. Go to the **Customers** page and switch the filter at the top to **Nonmembers**.
2. Open the profile of the user you want to restore.
3. In the **"This user is a nonmember"** banner at the top of the profile, press `Invite as member`.

Once invited:

- The user immediately appears on **Customers → Members** at this location.
- The user receives an email invitation to access the member web portal or mobile app.
- All their previous history — invoices, payments, credits, day passes, bookings, billing details, payment methods, internal notes, and attachments — remains intact.
- Administrators can now [create or edit a subscription](/admin/billing/subscriptions/edit-a-subscription) and manage other member properties on the profile.
