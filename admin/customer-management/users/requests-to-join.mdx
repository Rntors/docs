---
title: "Requests to join"
---

Learn how users can request to join a location as a member, and how administrators manage those requests.

Public users and [nonmembers](/admin/customer-management/users/nonmembers) can request to join a location as a [member](/admin/customer-management/users/members), and administrators can approve or reject their requests.

## Configure requests to join

1. Log in to RNTOR.
2. Open the necessary location and go to **Location settings → Customers**.
3. Manage the **Request to join** switch:
   - If **enabled**, public users will see the **Request to join** button and can submit requests.
   - If **disabled**, the **Request to join** button will not appear.

<Frame>
  ![Request to join setting](/images/placeholder-request-to-join-setting.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  If you have multiple locations, you can enable **Request to join** for some locations and disable it for others. The **Request to join** button will show up if it is enabled for at least one location, and users will only see the locations available for requests in the **Location** dropdown.
</Info>

## How users request to join

1. Public users and nonmembers who open and log in to the member portal or mobile apps can see the **Request to join** button in the top right corner of the **Locations** page.

<Frame>
  ![Request to join button on the Locations page](/images/placeholder-request-to-join-button.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  The **Request to join** button is only visible to users who are not members yet at any of your locations.
</Info>

2. Pressing the button opens a form to capture more information about the user and their request, such as full name, location of interest, and a short self-introduction.

<Frame>
  ![Request to join form](/images/placeholder-request-to-join-form.png)
</Frame>

3. When users submit the request, a notification is sent to the administrators of the selected location.

## How administrators manage requests to join

1. Administrators go to the **Customers** page in the relevant location and open the **Requests** tab to review all join requests.

<Frame>
  ![Requests tab on the Customers page](/images/placeholder-requests-tab.png)
</Frame>

2. Administrators open the request and approve or reject it.

<Frame>
  ![Approve or reject a join request](/images/placeholder-request-details.png)
</Frame>

3. If the request is approved, the user becomes a [member](/admin/customer-management/users/members) of the location and receives a welcome email. Learn more about [user roles](/admin/customer-management/users/user-roles).
