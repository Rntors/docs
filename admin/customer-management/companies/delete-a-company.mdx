## Delete a member company

If you want to remove a company from the **Customers** page because it no longer rents a space or its contract has ended, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Customers** → click the necessary company → press the menu (**…**) top right.
2. Choose one of the following options:
   - Press `Remove` if you want to move the company itself to **Nonmembers**, but keep its members on the **Customers** page under the **Users** tab.
   - Press `Remove with members` if you want to move the company together with all of its members to **Nonmembers**.

3. Confirm your choice.

### What happens when you remove a company from the Customers

- The company becomes a nonmember and appears for administrators on the **Customers** → **Nonmembers** page.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 08 At 8 59 47 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-08-at-8.59.47-PM.png)
</Frame>

- The company's active subscription is automatically deactivated.
- The company's [default payment method](/admin/billing/payments/payment-methods) remains attached for further charges if needed.
- The history of the company's credits, payments and invoices is preserved.
- The company disassociates from company members. Nonmember companies do not contain users.
- Administrators can [restore the company](#restore-a-company) at any time.

## Delete a nonmember company

If you want to remove companies that are no longer needed, or clear your database, administrators can permanently delete a nonmember company from the location by following these steps:

1. Go to **Customers** page.
2. Select the **Nonmembers** option in the drop-down list.
3. Click the necessary company to open its profile.
4. Press the menu (**…**) top right → select `Delete` → confirm your choice.

<Warning>
  Deleting a company from **Nonmembers** is irreversible and permanently removes it from the list. Administrators will no longer be able to access its past data or [restore the company](#restore-a-company) to the **Customers**.
</Warning>

## Restore a company

To move a company that appears on the nonmembers list back to the **Customers** page, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Customers** → menu (**…**) top right → **Nonmembers** tab.
2. Select a company and tap `Restore`.

Once restored, the company will reappear on the **Customers** → **Companies** tab. Administrators can add company members, create a subscription, and manage other company properties.

<Info>
  The company is restored to the **Customers** without the company users who belonged to the company earlier.
</Info>
