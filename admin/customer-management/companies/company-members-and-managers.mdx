---
title: "Company members and managers"
---

Your users can be grouped into companies. A company can have an unlimited number of company members and company managers.

This article will help you understand the difference between these two groups of users and provide insight into what they can do.

<Info>
  To learn how to manage company members and managers, please see the [Set up companies](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies) doc.
</Info>

## Who is a company member

A company member is a user who belongs to a specific company, can interact with all the resources assigned to their company, and share company subscription benefits, but cannot access or manage company billing.

## Who is a company manager

A company manager is a company user who is responsible for the company's billing, receives and pays company invoices, and can manage payment methods and other company settings.

## What a company manager and a member can do

### Subscription-related permissions

| Permissions | Company manager | Company member |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Use company credits, check credit balance and transactions | ✓ | Yes, [if enabled](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-payment-method-permissions) by a company manager |
| Use company day passes, check day pass balance and transactions | ✓ | Yes, [if enabled](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-payment-method-permissions) by a company manager |
| Use company [subscription discounts](/admin/billing/discounts/discounts-with-plan) | ✓ | ✓ |
| [Get access](/admin/integrations/access-control/set-up-access-control) to the space based on an access group in the company subscription | ✓ | ✓ |
| Access [resources assigned](/admin/resource-management/resources/manage-resource-rentals-with-assignments) to the company | ✓ | ✓ |

### Permissions to manage other company members

| Permissions | Company manager | Company member |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Invite new company members](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies#add-company-members-and-managers) | ✓ | ✗ |
| [Delete other members and managers from the company](/admin/customer-management/companies/set-up-companies#add-company-members-and-managers) | ✓ | ✗ |
| Create [room](/member/bookings/book-a-room)/[desk](/member/bookings/book-a-desk)/[parking](/member/bookings/book-a-parking-space) bookings for company members | ✓ | ✗ |

### Billing-related permissions 

| Permissions | Company manager | Company member |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Use the ["Company invoice" payment method](/admin/billing/payments/pay-with-invoice) to include payments in the company invoice | Yes, if [enabled](/admin/billing/payments/pay-with-invoice) by the administrator in the company subscription | Yes, if [enabled](https://help.spacebring.com/en/articles/9849600-customer-payment-method-permissions) by a company manager |
| [Manage company "Pay with invoice" options](/admin/billing/payments/pay-with-invoice) | Yes, if [enabled](/admin/billing/payments/pay-with-invoice) by the administrator in the company subscription | ✗ |
| Make payments with a company card | ✓ | Only if [enabled](https://help.spacebring.com/en/articles/9849600-customer-payment-method-permissions) by a company manager |
| [Purchase credit packages for a company](/admin/billing/credits-and-day-passes/packages) | ✓ | Only if a company manager [permits using the company card](https://help.spacebring.com/en/articles/9849600-customer-payment-method-permissions) for purchasing credit packages |
| View transactions made with a company card | ✓ | ✗ |
| View, receive, and [pay company invoices](/admin/billing/invoices/pay-for-invoice) | ✓ | ✗ |
| View company subscription details | ✓ | ✗ |
| [Manage company payment methods](/admin/billing/payments/payment-gateway) | ✓ | ✗ |
| [Manage company payment method permissions](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-payment-method-permissions) | ✓ | ✗ |
| [Add company billing details](/admin/customer-management/billing-details) | ✓ | ✗ |
| [Add company billing email](/admin/customer-management/billing) | ✓ | ✗ |
