title: "Billing"
---

Customers can access and manage their own billing data and settings in the customer portal, while certain aspects can only be managed by administrators from the admin dashboard.

## How administrators view and manage customer billing

To view and manage a customer's billing data and settings, administrators go to **Customers** \> open the user or company profile.

1. For companies, click **(…)** in the top right \> `Edit` to update the company name, logo, and contact details.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 09 At 2 00 29 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-09-at-2.00.29-PM.png)
</Frame>

2. Open the customer's **subscription** to:

- [Edit it](/admin/billing/subscriptions/edit-a-subscription) based on the new contract terms.
- [Pause payment collection](/admin/billing/subscriptions/control-invoice-issuance) for the customer.
- View the [upcoming invoice](/admin/billing/invoices/upcoming-invoices).

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 06 09 At 2 03 57 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-09-at-2.03.57-PM.png)
</Frame>

3. Go to the customer's profile to:

- Manage the **"[Pay with invoice](/admin/billing/payments/pay-with-invoice)"** setting via the company-invoice payment method and its [permissions](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-payment-method-permissions) (including the **None** option to disable a specific service entirely).
- [Manage the customer's invoices](/admin/billing/invoices/track-and-manage-invoices) and view [transactions](/admin/billing/payments/payment-gateway).
- Download invoices and receipts.
- Manage the customer's money balance (credits and day passes).
- Manage pending invoice items before the next invoice is issued.
- View the customer's payment methods and check whether they have a default one.
- Open the customer's payment history in Stripe via the **View in Stripe** button (available when the Stripe integration is connected).
- View or update the [customer's billing details](/admin/customer-management/users/billing-details).
- For companies, manage the billing email to send company invoices and receipts to an **additional recipient** apart from the company managers.
- Manage the **Auto-pay** setting.
- Enable and apply an alternative currency for this customer.

## How company managers view and manage their company billing

Company managers go to **Locations \> Membership \>** the section with their company name **\> Billing**. On this page, they can:

- View and pay company invoices.
- View transactions made with company payment methods.
- Download invoices and receipts.
- View their company's money balance (credits and day passes).
- Check their current subscription details.
- Check their upcoming invoice.
- Add a payment method, save it as default, change the default payment method, or delete a payment method.
- [Manage company payment method permissions](/admin/customer-management/companies/company-payment-method-permissions) for company cards, company invoice, credits, and day passes — including setting a service to **None** to disable that payment path entirely.
- Manage company [billing details](/admin/customer-management/users/billing-details).
- Manage the company billing email to send invoices and receipts to an additional recipient apart from the company managers.

## How individual users view and manage their billing

Individual users go to **Locations \> Membership \> Billing** under the "Personal" section. On this page, they can:

- View and pay their invoices, and view transactions.
- Download invoices and receipts.
- View their money balance.
- Check their current subscription details.
- Check their upcoming invoice.
- Add a payment method, save as default, change the default payment method, or delete a payment method.
- Manage their [billing details](/admin/customer-management/users/billing-details).
