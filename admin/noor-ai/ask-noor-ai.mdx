Instead of navigating through multiple menus or manually filtering lists, ask Noor AI to find information, summarize data, or pull specific reports for you.

## Start a chat with Noor AI

You can start a conversation with Noor AI directly from your admin dashboard:

1. Click the Noor AI icon in the top left corner.
2. Type your question or command into the message field or choose one of the suggested prompts.
3. Noor AI will process your request and provide the data, lists, or summaries you need right in the chat for you to review.

<Info>
  Noor AI can make mistakes. Ensure to double-check the information provided.
</Info>

## Tips for chatting with Noor AI

- Be specific. Instead of “show me bookings,” try “show me all bookings for ‘Meeting Room A’ between Oct 1 and Oct 5.”
- Refine your search. You can follow up on Noor AI’s answers. If Noor AI gives you a list of invoices, you can say, “Now only show me the ones issued on March 5th.”
