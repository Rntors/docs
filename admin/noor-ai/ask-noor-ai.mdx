Instead of navigating through multiple menus or manually filtering lists, ask Noor AI to find information, summarize data, or pull specific reports for you.

## Start a chat with Noor AI

You can start a conversation with Noor AI directly from your admin dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Noor AI">
    Click the Noor AI icon in the top left corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your request">
    Type your question or command into the message field or choose one of the suggested prompts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the response">
    Noor AI will process your request and provide the data, lists, or summaries you need right in the chat for you to review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Noor AI can make mistakes. Ensure to double-check the information provided.
</Info>

## What Noor AI can help with

Noor AI is connected to your entire workspace ecosystem. To get the best results, be specific with location names, date ranges, and formats.

### Bookings & resources

Check rooms, desks, or equipment availability without opening the calendar.

### Data formats & capabilities

Depending on your request, Noor AI can provide data in several helpful formats:

- **Summaries** — get a high-level overview of complex data. For example:

  <Card title="Summarize the status of all current contracts" />

- **Structured lists and tables** — Noor AI organizes data into easy-to-read lists and tables within the chat.
- **Pie and bar charts** — Noor AI creates different charts based on the retrieved data. For example:

  <CardGroup cols={1}>
    <Card title="Make a bar chart of bookings per day this month" />
    <Card title="Make a pie chart of benefits by category" />
  </CardGroup>

## Tips for chatting with Noor AI

- Be specific. Instead of “show me bookings,” try “show me all bookings for ‘Meeting Room A’ between Oct 1 and Oct 5.”
- Refine your search. You can follow up on Noor AI’s answers. If Noor AI gives you a list of invoices, you can say, “Now only show me the ones issued on March 5th.”
