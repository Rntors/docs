Noor AI can write posts for your **Feed**, whether it’s promoting a specific event, product, or benefit, or creating a post from scratch.

## Promote an event, product, or benefit

Promote your events, shop products, or benefits among customers with Noor AI. The **Promote** feature automatically generates a ready-to-use feed post to help you highlight specific new or existing items and attract more customer attention to them.

1. On the **Network settings > Noor AI** page, make sure you have **Noor AI** enabled.

2. Go to the **Events**, **Shop**, or **Benefits** page.

3. Create an event, product, or benefit, or choose an existing one.

4. Click the **(...)** menu next to it, then select **Promote**.

   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/images/noor-ai/promote-feed-item.png"
       alt="Selecting Promote for a shop product"
     />
   </Frame>

5. You’ll see a tab with an AI-generated feed post. The post is generated based on your event, product, or benefit details and includes its brief description, image (if added), and a direct link to join, order, or apply for it.

6. Feel free to make any edits, then click **Post** to publish it.

   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/images/noor-ai/review-promoted-feed-post.png"
       alt="Reviewing and publishing a feed post generated by Noor AI"
     />
   </Frame>

You can view and [edit the post](/engagement/feed) later via **Engagement > Feed**.

## Write a post from scratch

You can also ask Noor AI in chat to write a feed post for you.

1. Open **Noor AI** at the top left and start a chat.

2. Tell Noor AI what you want the post to say, including any details such as the message, tone, or images to include.

3. Noor AI will draft the post and share it with you for review.

4. Click **Approve** to publish it right away, or **Reject** if you don’t want to post it.

   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/images/noor-ai/create-feed-post-from-scratch.png"
       alt="Creating and approving a feed post from scratch with Noor AI"
     />
   </Frame>

<Tip>
  If you manage multiple locations, you can also ask Noor AI to publish the same post—or a tailored version—across several locations at once.
</Tip>
