## Platform Capabilities

Rntor provides APIs for managing:

- **Locations & Resources** - Meeting rooms, desks, offices, and equipment
- **Bookings** - Reservations with conflict detection and recurring options
- **Customers & Companies** - Member management with subscriptions and credits
- **Invoicing** - Automated billing and payment tracking
- **Events** - Community events with registration and check-in
